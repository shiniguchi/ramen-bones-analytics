## Overview

Ingest free external signals (weather, holidays, events), build a multi-horizon forecasting engine with conformal calibration and rolling-origin CV backtest gate, render forecast overlays on the revenue chart, attribute campaign uplift via ITS counterfactuals, and surface model quality transparently via ACCURACY-LOG. Ships the friend-owner a "did the campaign work?" answer backed by Track-B counterfactual CI, and gates future model promotions on ≥10% RMSE improvement vs a regressor-aware naive baseline.
