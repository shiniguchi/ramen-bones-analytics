### NEW Phase 16.1 decisions

#### Past-forecast visual on Calendar* cards (Area A)

- **D-01 — Past-forecast styling source-of-truth = mirror `RevenueForecastCard` / `InvoiceCountForecastCard` (Phase 15 v2).** The split-layer past/future pattern is already user-approved on the forecast cards; Calendar* cards become consistent siblings. Researcher MUST read `src/lib/components/RevenueForecastCard.svelte` and replicate the past/future layer split + styling on the Calendar* cards.
- **D-02 — Past-forecast lines: solid faded (~70% opacity); future-forecast lines: dashed (`stroke-dasharray='4 4'`).** Matches the backlog's Apple-Health-dotted-expected-line analogy. Per-model rendering preserved on both sides — all visible models in `visibleModels` Set render past+future lines (consistent with existing Calendar* card behavior). The current Spline rendering at `CalendarRevenueCard.svelte:327-337` already dashes naive_dow specifically — that styling collapses into the new past/future split: `naive_dow` past = solid faded gray, `naive_dow` future = dashed gray; other models past = solid faded model-color, future = dashed model-color.
- **D-03 — `chartXDomain` stays `today + 365d`.** Existing auto-scroll to "today" at ~60% viewport (`CalendarRevenueCard.svelte:209-263`) is unchanged. Past-forecast styling alone closes the perceived-gap problem; layout shock avoided.
- **D-04 — CI Area band continuous across past + future at `fill-opacity={0.06}`.** Owner sees the model's uncertainty cone covering both "what we said" and "what we predict" — continuity reinforces the "lag region is just data we haven't uploaded" mental model.

#### CampaignUpliftCard hero copy regime tiering (Area B)

- **D-05 — 3 maturity tiers driven by `headline.row.n_days` from `/api/campaign-uplift`.** Tiers: `<14d` / `14-28d` / `>=28d`. `n_days` is already in the API payload (Phase 16 D-08), server-side truth from `cumulative_uplift.py`. No client-side date math.
- **D-06 — Tier × CI-overlaps-zero matrix (5 distinct headline strings per locale).** `<14d` collapses to a single string regardless of CI state — too early for any claim. Mature tiers branch by CI:

  | Tier | CI overlaps zero | CI doesn't overlap (positive `cumulative_uplift_eur > 0`) | CI doesn't overlap (negative) |
  |---|---|---|---|
  | `<14d` | "Too early to tell" | "Too early to tell" | "Too early to tell" |
  | `14-28d` | "Probably not measurable yet" | "Looks like the campaign added revenue, but more weeks of data would confirm" | "Looks like the campaign reduced revenue, but more weeks of data would confirm" |
  | `>=28d` | "No measurable lift after N weeks" (N = `Math.floor(n_days/7)`) | "Yes, your campaign appears to have added revenue" | "Yes, your campaign appears to have reduced revenue" |

  i18n key suggestion (planner finalizes): `uplift_hero_too_early`, `uplift_hero_early_not_measurable`, `uplift_hero_early_added`, `uplift_hero_early_reduced`, `uplift_hero_mature_no_lift`, `uplift_hero_mature_added`, `uplift_hero_mature_reduced` — 7 keys total (5 distinct strings, with `<14d` cell sharing one key across all CI states; planner may collapse the matrix differently).
- **D-07 — Tone register: hedged in `<14d` / `14-28d`; direct ("Yes / No") in `>=28d`.** Calibrates confidence to data maturity. Friend-owner sees uncertainty conveyed without statistical jargon.

#### Statistical detail disclosure (Area C)

- **D-08 — Plain-language secondary line under hero.** Replaces the current `−€565 (95% CI −€3,745 ... +€2,298)` subtitle with prose like "Best estimate: ~€565 less revenue than expected, but the range covers anywhere from €3,700 less to €2,300 more — that's normal day-to-day noise." Numbers come from `headline.row.cumulative_uplift_eur` / `ci_lower_eur` / `ci_upper_eur` (rounded to whole EUR, German thousand separators per existing `formatEur` at `CampaignUpliftCard.svelte:127-131`). Tone follows the regime tier from D-06.
- **D-09 — Inline "How is this calculated? ›" text-link disclosure trigger.** Below the sparkline. Tap toggles a collapsible panel inline within the same card. Standard mobile pattern — no modal, no bottom sheet, no new component infrastructure. Chevron rotates on toggle (`›` → `⌄`).
- **D-10 — Disclosure panel content.** Three sections inside the panel:
  1. **Statistical numbers** — current "−€565 (95% CI −€3,745 ... +€2,298)" line, kept verbatim for power users / methodology checks.
  2. **Anticipation buffer note** — current "Counterfactual fits on data ≥7 days before the campaign start" copy, plain-language version: "We compare your actual revenue against what the model predicted from data 7+ days before the campaign launched, so any pre-launch anticipation isn't counted as campaign uplift."
  3. **Naive disagreement note** (only when `divergenceWarning === true`) — current "Naive baseline disagrees — review the methodology" replaced with plain-language: "Two of our checks disagree — we'd want more weeks of data before drawing conclusions."
- **D-11 — Default state: collapsed, no localStorage persistence.** Mobile-first; respects "card is for the friend." No power-user toggle; if owner wants stats every time, she taps every time — a small cost vs. the simplicity win.
