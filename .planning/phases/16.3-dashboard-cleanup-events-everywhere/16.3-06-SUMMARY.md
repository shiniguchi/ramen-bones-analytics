## Reactivity guarantee verified by reading the diff

The cache-key widening is the load-bearing change. Before the change, `if (lastFetchedGrain === g) return;` would short-circuit any rangeStart update at unchanged grain. After: `if (lastFetchedKey === key) return;` where `key = g|rs` re-fires the fetch on EITHER axis change. This was the trap CONTEXT.md flagged ("range_start works the first time but goes stale on later updates").
