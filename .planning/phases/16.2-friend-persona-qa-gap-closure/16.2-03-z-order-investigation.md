## Conclusion

**Path 3 — no code change needed.**

The owner's "lines go behind the bars sometimes" complaint does NOT reproduce as a literal z-order bug. SVG paint order = source order; Splines are after Bars in source; DOM inspection confirms paths come after rects. Splines render visually on top per spec.

The visual perception of "Spline behind bar" is attributable to:
1. **Past Spline strokeOpacity={0.7}** (16.1-01 D-02 design) makes the line faded against dense colored bar fills. The line IS painted on top, but at 70% opacity it visually blends into the bar fill.
2. **Stroke width** at strokeWidth=1 for naive_dow makes it harder to see against thick bars.
3. **Item 1 freeze** — the perf hang during filter changes may have produced transient stale render states where Splines appeared to lag behind bar redraws. Plan 16.2-01's dual-cascade fix (4221ms → 1219ms blocking) substantially reduces this stale-render window.

**No reorder of `<Bars>` / `<Spline>` / `<Area>` blocks applied.** No strokeOpacity/strokeWidth bump applied — those are 16.1-01 design decisions and changing them risks regressing other dashboard cards' visual consistency. If the friend re-encounters the issue post-fix, surface it as a separate v1.4 polish task with a specific bucket repro for targeted treatment.
